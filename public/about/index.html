About

Plumas Disc Golf is a non-profit organization dedicated to furthering the sport of disc golf in Plumas County, CA.

PDG was founded by Jeremiah Bridges and Richard Dolezal in 2018. Our first course was Pioneer Park Disc Golf Course in Quincy, which was made possible by the help of James Shipp of Central Plumas Recreation and Parks District, and we have hopes of opening other courses in the area.

We run tournaments, leagues, fundraisers, and other events to further awareness and participation in the sport we love, disc golf. We outreach using traditional and social media avenues, including word of mouth, flyers, tweets, Facebook posts, and more.

About Plumas County

What's your outdoor passion? Winter sports? Snowmobiling? Skiing? Mountain biking? Fishing? Camping? Golf? Scenic drives? Relaxation? We have it all.

There are lots of Places to Stay all around the county, from high-end villas to small motels with friendly local staff. Most campgrounds and RV parks are open, so just click on Places to Camp to find an ideal spot for you. Plumas County is still virtually unknown by many, once you discover the county and visit you will return. Plumas County is an easy drive from the Bay Area, Sacramento, Reno, and Tahoe. Once here you can sit back and relax or choose to challenge yourself, hiking, mountain biking, fishing, horseback riding, or off-road adventures. For the motorcyclist we have some of the best roads to travel with great scenery. The businesses of Plumas County are waiting to serve you and make your visit memorable!
